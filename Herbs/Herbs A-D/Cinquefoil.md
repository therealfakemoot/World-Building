>#  Cinquefoil
>
> **Locale:** Rural
> **Climate:** Temperate
> **Availability:** Summer 10%
> **Ability Check:** Intelligence -5
> **Uses:** 1
> **Value:** 10 gp/ 15 gp
> **Preparation:** 1 hour
> **Description:** This plant creeps along the ground on long slender stringlike stalks. It has five or seven part leaves, and small yellow flowers.
